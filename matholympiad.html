Math Olympiad

I used to take part in the Mathematical Olympiad, related events and correspondence seminars PraSe and iKS. Sometimes I contribute to the organization and problem selection of the Czech Mathematical Olympiad. In the last three years, together with my colleagues, I organized the Czech-Polish-Slovak-Austrian Match at ISTA.
